## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is TangentCore free?**
A: Yes, the alpha version is completely free.

**Q: Do I need special permissions?**
A: You need a Google Workspace account with access to Google Drive.

**Q: Can I use this on personal Google accounts?**
A: Currently designed for Google Workspace, but may work on personal accounts.

### Scanning Questions

**Q: How long does a scan take?**
A: Usually 10-30 seconds for up to 500 files.

**Q: Why is there a 500-file limit?**
A: Alpha version cap to ensure fast performance. Will increase in Beta.

**Q: What if I have more than 500 shared files?**
A: The scan will analyze the first 500 files found.

**Q: Can I scan specific folders?**
A: Not in alpha version. Coming in future updates.

### Results Questions

**Q: Why don't I see any results?**
A: You may not have any files shared with you, or they may be very small.

**Q: Are the sizes accurate?**
A: Yes, sizes are pulled directly from Google Drive API.

**Q: Can I export the results?**
A: Not in alpha version. Export feature coming in Beta.

### Action Questions

**Q: Can I delete files from TangentCore?**
A: Not in alpha version. This is read-only for safety. Deletion coming in Beta.

**Q: How do I manually clean up files?**
A: Go to Google Drive â†’ "Shared with me" â†’ Review and remove files.

**Q: Will deleted files affect others?**
A: Removing a shared file only removes it from YOUR view, not the original.
